Publications

A Deep Approach for Volumetric Tractography Segmentation

Published in: Pattern Recognition and Image Analysis, IbPRIA 2023, Lecture Notes in Computer Science, vol. 14062. Springer, Cham

Authors: Pablo Rocamora García, Miguel Ángel Lozano, Jorge Azorín-López

Abstract:

Tractography is a technique that allows the reconstruction of white matter tracts from diffusion-weighted magnetic resonance imaging. The segmentation of these tracts is a challenging task due to their complex three-dimensional structure. In this paper, we present a novel deep learning approach for volumetric tractography segmentation using 3D convolutional neural networks. Our method transforms streamline representations into volumetric density maps and employs a specialized architecture to effectively capture the spatial relationships between different white matter tracts. Experimental results demonstrate that our approach achieves state-of-the-art performance on benchmark datasets, outperforming traditional methods in both accuracy and computational efficiency.
